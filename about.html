About Me

I am a PhD Scholar in the Department of Electrical, Electronics and Communication Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Dharwad. My research interests focus on VLSI design, AI hardware accelerators, and energy-efficient computing architectures.

I completed my M.Tech in VLSI Design and Embedded Systems and my Bachelor’s degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering. I have hands-on experience in digital hardware, ASIC design concepts, and AI-based system development.
