Research Interests

Projects

AI-Based Crop Disease Detection System

Developed a CNN-based system for early identification of crop diseases using image processing. The project focused on model training and hardware-aware AI deployment strategies.

Design and Analysis of CMOS Low Noise Amplifier

Designed and analyzed a CMOS LNA using source inductive degeneration to improve noise performance and gain. Simulations were carried out using Cadence Virtuoso.

Smart Street Lighting System

Implemented an IoT-based smart street lighting system using Arduino for energy-efficient lighting control.
